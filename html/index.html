Purely Balanced pH

pH levels can greatly affect the taste of water.
We use water purification, addition of electrolytes to consistently
deliver the fresh and pure taste of BaubAqua water. We closely monitor
our pH levels to ensure we're providing our customers with a consistent
product that is purely balanced.

Electrolytes

Water shouldn't on;y hydrate, it should taste good, too.
Electrolytes are ionized minerals such as potassium,calcium, and magnessium,
and the unique blend of electrolytes in BaubaAqua water creates a taste that is
distictly fresh and pure.
